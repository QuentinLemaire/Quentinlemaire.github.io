About me

As long as I can remember, I have always been keen to artistic activities. I started using the Adobe suite when I was only 12. I never stopped exploring the endless possibilities of it ever since.

Nowadays, I bloom working in visual communication. From tech startups to institutional organisations, I challenged myself through various professional circles where I learnt so much. I will forever be grateful for all the people who gave me a chance to try and to learn.

Always looking for new adventures, I’d be glad to exchanges works with you if you have any challenges for me.

IAE FRANCE
Annual Report

As a Communication Officer Junior for IAE FRANCE, I was in charge of the creation fo the first ever Annual Report. The fourty-pages document had to be design from scratch, fitting the IAE FRANCE strong brand identity.

Designed in Adobe Indesign, and printed by a professional printer, the decoument was distributed to a wide range of people, from journalists to Ministers.
